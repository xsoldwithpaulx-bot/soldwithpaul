Frequently Asked Questions — Choosing a Realtor in Pasadena

How do I find the best realtor in Pasadena?
Start by reviewing their recent transaction history in Pasadena specifically through MLS-syndicated portals like Zillow, Realtor.com, or Redfin. Look for agents with strong list-to-sold ratios, low average days on market, and positive client reviews. Pasadena's mix of historic craftsman bungalows, Prairie-style estates, and newer developments rewards agents with architectural knowledge. Ask about their marketing plan, pricing strategy, and communication style during an initial consultation.
How much do realtors charge in Pasadena, CA?
Real estate commission in California is negotiable but typically ranges from 5% to 6% of the sale price, split between the listing and buyer's agents. Some agents offer alternative commission structures. Given Pasadena's elevated median prices, the total commission should be weighed against the agent's ability to maximize your sale price — a skilled agent who achieves a higher sale price more than offsets their commission.
Should I use a local Pasadena realtor or a big-name agent?
Local expertise matters significantly in Pasadena's market. An agent with deep knowledge of Pasadena neighborhoods — from Bungalow Heaven and Madison Heights to the Arroyo and San Rafael hills — can often achieve better results than a high-volume agent covering a large territory. Look for agents who actively list and sell in Pasadena and can speak to block-level pricing differences between historic districts and newer zones.
What questions should I ask when interviewing a listing agent in Pasadena?
Ask about their recent sales in Pasadena specifically, their pricing strategy for the current market, their marketing plan (photography, staging, digital marketing), their average days on market, their list-to-sold ratio, and how they handle multiple offers. Request references from past Pasadena clients and verify their listings through the MLS.
How long does it take to sell a home in Pasadena?
The average days on market in Pasadena varies by season and market conditions. Current data is available on our Pasadena market data page. Working with a top-performing agent can significantly reduce time on market through strategic pricing and effective marketing.
