About Paul

Your Southern California Listing Specialist

I have been a licensed California Realtor since 2008 and full-time since 2012. Based in Whittier and affiliated with NexGen Realtors in Rancho Cucamonga, I serve homeowners across Los Angeles, Orange, Riverside, and San Bernardino counties.

My focus is residential seller representation. I believe every homeowner deserves an agent who treats their home like it is the most important listing on the market — because to you, it is.

Over 186 transactions have taught me that the best results come from a combination of accurate pricing, professional presentation, and proactive communication. My career list-to-sold ratio of 103% and average days on market of 25 days reflect that commitment.

I am not the agent who will pressure you into a decision. I am the agent who will give you the data, the strategy, and the honest guidance to make the best decision for your family. When you are ready, I am here.
