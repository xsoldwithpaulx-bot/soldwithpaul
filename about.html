Paul Fernandez

Real estate, without the noise.

Licensed since 2008 and full-time since 2012. Based in Whittier, affiliated with NexGen Realtors in Rancho Cucamonga, and serving homeowners across Los Angeles, Orange, Riverside, and San Bernardino counties.

My focus is residential seller representation — first homes, simultaneous moves, investment properties, and inherited homes settled during difficult seasons. The goal is clarity before you decide.

Over 200+ transactions have taught me that the best results come from a combination of accurate pricing, professional presentation, and proactive communication. The math on this is straightforward, and the numbers reflect it: a career list-to-sold ratio of 103% and an average of 25 days on market.

I'm not the agent who will pressure you into a decision. I'm the advisor who will walk through the data, name the tradeoffs, and explain what comes next. When you're ready, I'm here.
