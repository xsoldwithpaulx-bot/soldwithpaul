Frequently Asked Questions — Choosing a Realtor in Claremont

How do I find the best realtor in Claremont?
Start by reviewing their recent transaction history in Claremont specifically through MLS-syndicated portals like Zillow, Realtor.com, or Redfin. Look for agents with strong list-to-sold ratios, low average days on market, and positive client reviews. Claremont's mix of historic homes, mid-century properties, and newer developments rewards agents with architectural knowledge. Ask about their marketing plan, pricing strategy, and communication style during an initial consultation.
How much do realtors charge in Claremont, CA?
Real estate commission in California is negotiable but typically ranges from 5% to 6% of the sale price, split between the listing and buyer's agents. Some agents offer alternative commission structures. Given Claremont's elevated median prices, the total commission should be weighed against the agent's ability to maximize your sale price — a skilled agent who achieves a higher sale price more than offsets their commission.
Should I use a local Claremont realtor or a big-name agent?
Local expertise matters significantly in Claremont's market. An agent with deep knowledge of Claremont's historic Village, College-adjacent neighborhoods, Claraboya, and newer foothill developments can often achieve better results than a high-volume agent covering a large territory. Look for agents who actively list and sell in Claremont and can speak to block-level pricing differences between the Village and the surrounding zones.
What questions should I ask when interviewing a listing agent in Claremont?
Ask about their recent sales in Claremont specifically, their pricing strategy for the current market, their marketing plan (photography, staging, digital marketing), their average days on market, their list-to-sold ratio, and how they handle multiple offers. Request references from past Claremont clients and verify their listings through the MLS.
How long does it take to sell a home in Claremont?
The average days on market in Claremont varies by season and market conditions. Current data is available on our Claremont market data page. Working with a top-performing agent can significantly reduce time on market through strategic pricing and effective marketing.
